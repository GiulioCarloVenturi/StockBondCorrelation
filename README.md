# HF Stock-Bond Correlation
## Goal
Compute stock-bond correlation with high-frequency future data
## Content
1. Update and matain the archive of high-frequency future data for stock indices and government bonds
2. Compute and update intra-day high-frequency correlations between future prices of stock indices and government bonds
3. Plot long-run correlations (20-day moving average) ![alt text](https://github.com/GiulioCarloVenturi/StockBondCorrelation/blob/main/StockBondCorrelation_USEA_hist.png)
4. Plot correlations during the last 6 months (EA) ![alt text](https://github.com/GiulioCarloVenturi/StockBondCorrelation/blob/main/StockBondCorrelation2Y_EA.png)
